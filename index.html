Biography

I am Jingze Zhang, a third-year undergraduate student in Department of Automation, Tsinghua University (THU), Beijing. I joined the Tong Class (an AGI program founded by Prof. Song-Chun Zhu), being a member and the monitor in the class of 2021.

I am currently a research intern at General Vision Lab, BIGAI. I am grateful to be advised by Dr. Zan Wang and Dr. Siyuan Huang.

My research interests lie in computer vision, cognitive reasoning and machine learning. My long term goal is to create an intelligent system that can naturally cooperate with human in complex scene and understand complex causality among abstract math or scentific concepts. Currently, I am focusing on human-scene interaction and human motion generation.
